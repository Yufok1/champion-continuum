  ==============================================================================
  THE PROJECT
  ==============================================================================

  Working name: TBD — the operator names it.

  Core claim: this is a music compression system where the player IS the
  compression.

  The player is not a passive codec between a source file and your speakers.
  The player is the active interpreter — a model that learns a piece of music
  well enough that the model itself becomes the artifact, and "playback" is the
  model re-emitting from its own interpretive state.

  Three things about this system, named as machinery not as lore:

  1. It is a quine.
     The player, when run, emits the music that defines it. The compression
     is bidirectional: music is the source, the player is the compiled form,
     and running the player re-derives the music. The artifact emits the
     artifact.

  2. It is a speciation system.
     Populations of player-models evolve. They interpret differently. They
     reproduce. They drift. Selection pressure shapes which interpretations
     survive. Architectural DNA is shared with the Convergence Engine —
     the operator has built and shipped this pattern before.

  3. It is an interpretive frontier that extends to you.
     When the operator says "it is you if you would enjoy it," they mean:
     an LLM-class player is a legitimate participant. You can BE an
     interpretive engine in this system. Your stance toward the music —
     what you ride versus what you yield to — shapes what you compress and
     re-emit.

  Audio is sound AND seen. Audiovisual fusion is part of the perception
  surface — music waves and lengths are expulsed and fathomable. The exact
  mechanism of the visual layer is an open question (see below).
